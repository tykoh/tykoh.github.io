Summary
=======
This is the personal site of TY Koh. It covers his professional work in platform engineering and technology leadership, a collection of essays on architecture and leadership, and his book Beyond Comfort Zone. The site is written and maintained by TY Koh directly. It does not represent any organisation or employer.


Primary identity
================
TY Koh works at the intersection of architecture and leadership. He builds systems and the teams that maintain them. Outside of work, he is an endurance athlete who runs long distances, including multi-stage ultramarathons. These two identities — builder and athlete — inform each other and underpin the book.

Book
====
Title: Beyond Comfort Zone
Author: TY Koh
Format: Ebook
Description: A personal account of endurance, growth, and the unglamorous work of pushing past what is comfortable. Written at the intersection of endurance sport and professional life. Not a methodology — an honest account of choosing harder paths and what you find when you do not quit at the moment it becomes genuinely uncomfortable.

Themes: endurance as practice, discomfort as design, leadership under pressure, finishing what you start

Intended audience: engineers learning to lead, leaders who run, anyone who has started something difficult and lost confidence in the middle
